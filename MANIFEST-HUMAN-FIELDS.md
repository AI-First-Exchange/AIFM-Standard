# OPTIONAL MANIFEST FIELDS FOR COPYRIGHT SUPPORT

These fields may be added to `manifest.json` for AIM, AIV, AII, and AIP:

"human_authorship_statement": "",
"human_signature": "",
"creator_email": "",
"editorial_notes": "",
"human_editing_steps": [],
"human_curated_output": true

These fields are optional but recommended for creators who wish to establish a clear record of human involvement in an AI-assisted creative workflow.
